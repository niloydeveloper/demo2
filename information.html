Information About e-Visa For Uganda

The e-Visa for Uganda

The e-Visa for Uganda was introduced on April 2006 by the Government of Uganda, to enable travelers to make an easy and quick application for a Uganda e-Visa for visiting the country of Uganda for tourism or business purposes. A foreign citizen from any of one of the eligible countries can apply online for an e-Visa before departure.

Which are the Uganda e-Visa Entry Specifications, Validity and Length of Authorized Stay?

The e-Visa for Uganda is allowing its holder to visit the country of Uganda in one single entry for a maximum period of time of thirty (30) days. The e-Visa for Uganda is allowing its holder to visit the country of Uganda with one entry and one exit, per each time; depending on each application with its needs and characteristics. This will be determined at the exact moment of application. The e-Visa for Uganda is valid for a period of thirty (30) days.

Passport guidelines for the e-Visa for Uganda?

Elegible countries

Travelers that are Citizens and nationals from the following countries listed below are eligible to apply online for a Uganda e-Visa. Including the Germany, United Kingdom, Italy, United States, Netherlands, Austria, France, and Czech Republic.

Additional evidence requirements

In addition to the personal information and travel details that are provided during the online form, applicants may perfectly be required to provide the following document:

A scanned copy or picture of the biographical page of the current valid passport that has been used during the online application form. The applicant can perfectly submit this required information in a later stage and does not need to provide this mandatory document at the moment of application.

Documentation Needed at Uganda International Ports of Entry Any Tourist traveling with an Uganda e-Visa are required to present the following documentation upon arrival at a Uganda International port of entry:

Printed approved e-Visa; Passport valid for at least 6 months (it has to be the same one used to complete the e-Visa application form);

Verify Status of Uganda e-Visa

Uganda e-Visa applicants will receive any future notification and updates about the status of their application delivered to their inbox. If it is finally approved, the e-Visa for Uganda will be sent to the applicant to his inbox directly via email.
