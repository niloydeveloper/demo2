Application for Online e-Visa for Uganda

Any Foreign eligible individuals that are planning to visit Uganda are able to complete a short and easy online e-Visa application form for travelling to Uganda for tourism purposes.

This online process for e-Visa for Uganda was implemented towards the 1st of July of 2016 by the Government of Uganda, with the goal of enabling any of the future eligible travelers to apply for an e-Visa to Uganda.

The e-Visa for Uganda

The e-Visa for Uganda is a document granted by the Government of Uganda that is allowing the
citizens of any of the eligible countries with
passports that are valid for at least six (6) months from the expected date of entry into Uganda, to apply online for any of the two types of Uganda e-Visa for tourism purposes. The Uganda e-Visa allows the single or the multiple entry.
